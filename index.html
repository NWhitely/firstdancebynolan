Meet Nolan

Hi, I'm Nolan Wayne, a professional dancer specializing in Ballroom, Country, Swing, and more. I have a passion for helping couples create unforgettable wedding moments.

I know that stepping onto the dance floor in front of everyone can feel intimidating. That's why I've made it my mission to make the choreography process fun, stress-free, and completely tailored to you. Whether you want a classic waltz, a country two-step, a smooth swing, or something uniquely yours, I'll create the perfect dance for your big moment.

Every couple has their own story, and your first dance should tell yours. I'm proud to work with all couples, regardless of gender or orientation. Let's create something beautiful together.

Most couples book 4-6 sessions, which gives us enough time to learn the choreography and build confidence. If you're looking for something simpler (a classic sway with a few highlights), 2-3 sessions might be enough. For a show-stopping routine with lifts or complex moves, plan for 6-8 sessions. I recommend starting lessons 2-3 months before your wedding. This gives us plenty of time to learn, practice, and polish without feeling rushed. That said, I've worked with couples as close as 3 weeks out, and we just adjust expectations accordingly. The sooner you book, the more flexibility we have with scheduling.
